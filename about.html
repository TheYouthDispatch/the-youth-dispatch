About The Youth Dispatch

A publication built by students—for students— dedicated to thoughtful journalism, civic engagement, educational resources, and meaningful research.

✦ ✦ ✦

Our Mission

The Youth Dispatch is an independent, youth-led publication focused on civic engagement, public policy, psychology, media literacy, culture, and the social sciences.

We believe young people deserve access to thoughtful journalism, educational resources, and opportunities that encourage curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Our publication exists to provide students with a platform to publish research, opinion pieces, interviews, and analysis while making high-quality educational content accessible to everyone.
